4-Bit AC Nano-Processor

Rectifier-less, Battery-free Computing for Smart Dust

A revolutionary adiabatic processor deriving power and data synchronization directly from RF fields for implantable biomedical devices.

Back to Projects

The Power Bottleneck

Traditional wireless power transfer (WPT) is limited by the inefficiency of RF-to-DC rectifiers and the massive silicon area required for storage capacitors. For "Smart Dust" and internal biomedical implants, these components are too bulky and power-hungry.

Our project solves this by eliminating the rectifier entirely, using Quadrature RF-Only (QRF) logic to process information in the phase domain.

AC-Logic & QRF Families

We utilize the inherent adiabatic nature of the proposed logic cells to achieve ultra-low power consumption:

  • Phase-Based States: Logic 0 and 1 are represented by 0° and 180° phases respectively.
  • QRF Timing: Quadrature (I and Q) signals manage Pre-charge, Hold, and Evaluate phases.
  • Nano-Watt Consumption: Fundamental gates (NOT, NAND, NOR) validated at 8.2 nW to 20.8 nW in 65nm CMOS.

Implementation Pipeline

  1. RF Energy Harvesting: Direct AC power extraction without DC conversion.
  2. Phase Synchronization: Deriving timing directly from the transmitted RF field.
  3. 4-Bit Core: Processing logic via AC-Logic cells for arithmetic and control.
  4. Ultra-Low Power Layout: Optimized 65nm CMOS cells for minimal leakage and maximum adiabatic gain.
  5. Tapeout Ready: US Patent filed; currently refining layout for silicon fabrication.

Key Advantages

  • Battery-Free: Ideal for environments where battery replacement is impossible (e.g., deep tissue).
  • Size Reduction: Eliminates bulky storage capacitors and heavy rectifiers.
  • Privacy & Longevity: Secure, "in-house" computing for long-term autonomous sensing.
