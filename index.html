About the Summit

The Pakistan Semiconductor Summit 2026 presents tapeout-ready ASIC design projects developed by graduate students at the IC Design Lab. Projects span digital VLSI architectures, mixed-signal circuits, and cutting-edge semiconductor research targeting real silicon fabrication.

Each project is mentored by faculty experts, leveraging professional EDA tools such as Cadence Virtuoso, Innovus, and Synopsys for synthesis, verification, and physical design. The summit emphasizes innovation, collaboration, and preparing chips ready for tapeout.
